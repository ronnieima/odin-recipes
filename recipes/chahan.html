Oishi Chahan

This is a quick and easy all-in-one meal...has rice, meat, and vegetables.

If you want a little more meat, you can match it up with some chicken teriyaki or grilled barbecue ribs...yum!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup minced carrot
  • 1 cup thinly sliced green beans
  • 1 medium white onion, minced
  • 3 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces ground beef
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 ½ cups cooked white rice
  • 1 large egg
  • ¾ cup soy sauce (such as Kikkoman®), or to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large saute pan over medium-low heat. Saute carrots, green beans, onion, and garlic in the hot butter until onions are transparent, 5 to 7 minutes. Add ground beef to the vegetables and season with salt and pepper. Cook and stir until ground beef is browned and crumbly and carrots are softened, 5 to 7 minutes. Add rice and mix well.
  2. Crack the egg into a bowl and beat using a fork. Mix into rice mixture until egg is no longer runny, 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Stir in soy sauce. Taste and add more soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Mix rice well. When you're satisfied with the seasoning, turn off the heat and serve.
